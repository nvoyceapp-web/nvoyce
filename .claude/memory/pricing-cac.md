## Pricing Model

**Free Plan:** 3 invoices/month (freemium entry point, removes friction)

**Pro Plan:** $19/month (unlimited invoices, all features)
- Target: Growth-phase freelancers (5-20 clients, $2K-10K/mo)
- ROI: Save 5 hours/month + get paid 5-10 days faster

**Business Plan:** $39/month (team features, up to 5 members)
- Target: Small agencies

## Competitive Positioning

| Tool | Price | Focus | Gap |
|------|-------|-------|-----|
| Wave | Free | Generic invoicing | Ad-supported, no AI |
| Invoicer.ai | $10-50 | Broad SMB suite | Not AI-native, not gig-focused |
| **Nvoyce** | **$19** | **AI + gig workers** | **Differentiated** |
| Bonsai | $39-99 | Enterprise suite | Expensive, too many features |

**Why $19:** Premium signal (vs $10), undercuts Bonsai ($39), clear ROI

**Why 3 invoices free:** Enough to experience value, creates conversion trigger

## Economics

**Break-Even:** 26 paying customers
- Fixed costs: ~$450/month (Vercel, Supabase, Claude, Stripe, Resend, domain)
- Variable cost per customer: ~$2/month
- Unit economics: ($19 - $2) = $17 gross margin per customer
- Break-even: $450 ÷ $17 = 26 customers

**Target:** 50 paying by May 31 (2x break-even)
- 50 × $19 = $950 MRR (plus Business plan upgrades)

**CAC & LTV:**
- CAC (Cost to Acquire): <$20 (organic + ProductHunt)
- LTV (12 months): $228 (conservative, no churn)
- LTV:CAC Ratio: 11:1 (strong)
- Payback Period: 6 days

## Retention Strategy

**Engagement Tactics:**
- Welcome email with quick-start guide
- Weekly email: "Your Payme insights" (if unpaid invoices)
- Monthly email: "Revenue collected, growth trends"
- In-app alerts for proposals expiring or invoices overdue

**Win-Back (30-day inactive):**
- "Your clients owe you money, come back"
- Custom offer: 50% off next month

**Target Retention:**
- Day 7: 60% return
- Day 30: 40% return
- Month 3: 25% active

## Expansion Revenue (Phase 4+)

**Upsell Opportunities:**
- Advanced Payme ($29/month): ML insights, rate optimization
- Integrations ($10/month each): Slack, SMS, QuickBooks
- Team member additions: $10/month per person

**Expected:** Year 1 = 80% Pro, 20% expansions. Year 2+ = 60% Pro, 40% expansions + Business tier
